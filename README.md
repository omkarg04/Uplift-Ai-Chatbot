## ✨ About

Uplift is a conversational AI companion designed to be a warm, first point of contact for people going through emotional struggles — stress, anxiety, sadness, or just needing to talk something through. Rather than replacing professional support, it's built to listen, validate, and gently guide users toward a more grounded headspace.

## 💬 Features

- **Empathetic Chat Interface** — A conversational, back-and-forth chat experience powered by a large language model
- **Emotionally-Aware Responses** — Prompted to respond with warmth, active listening, and encouragement rather than generic advice
- **No-Code AI Architecture** — Built entirely on Amazon PartyRock, chaining foundation models through prompt-based widgets
- **Instant Access** — No login or setup required; open the link and start talking

## 🛠️ Built With

- **[Amazon PartyRock](https://partyrock.aws/)** — No-code generative AI app builder
- **Amazon Bedrock Foundation Models** — Underlying LLM powering the conversational responses
- **Prompt Engineering** — Custom-tuned system prompts to shape an empathetic, supportive conversational tone

## 🚀 Usage

1. Open the [live app](https://partyrock.aws/u/iomkar04/u7zZylGVQ/Uplift%253A-Your-Emotional-Companion)
2. Type how you're feeling or what's on your mind into the chat
3. Uplift responds with supportive, empathetic dialogue to help you process your thoughts

No installation, sign-up, or API key needed — it runs entirely in-browser via PartyRock.

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

Uplift is an AI chatbot intended for **casual emotional support and reflection only**. It is **not a substitute for professional mental health care**. If you or someone you know is in crisis or experiencing suicidal thoughts, please reach out to a licensed mental health professional or a crisis helpline in your region immediately.
